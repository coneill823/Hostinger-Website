About Me

Biomedical engineer, problem-solver, and hands-on tinkerer.

Hi, I'm Cameron — a biomedical engineer who recently completed a BEng at the University of Glasgow. I'm drawn to innovative environments where engineering advances patient care, and I enjoy working through complex problems and the challenges they bring. I'm especially interested in antibody engineering and therapeutic development, alongside the medical devices and rehabilitation work that first pulled me into the field.

My degree spanned tissue and cell engineering, biosensors and diagnostics, signal processing, rehabilitation engineering, and analogue and microelectronics. Outside the coursework I've built up hands-on R&D experience in prosthetics and orthotics, volunteered on projects that put engineering to social use, and I code for fun in my spare time.
